About Me

I'm Chris Hailey. A web developer from Memphis, Tennessee with a passion for connecting the community. My first loves were live music and the outdoors... both for their unique ability to make you feel connected to something greater. They can bring a community together. This is what I aim to accomplish as a web developer. Through clear cut, visually pleasing and engaging design, we can build a platform that leaves people feeling connected to something greater.
THIS has been my pursuit for the last three years of my life. I've been travelling the world to learn, listen and connect with those that are different from me. Now I aim to bring these communities together through innovative web design.
